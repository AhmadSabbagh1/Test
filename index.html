About Me

I am Sarah Muhannad, a Syrian national, born on July 21, 2003, in Damascus. My current residence is in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.

I am a girl who loves success and enjoys seeing everyone around me happy. I pursue my dreams and am very humble with my followers. I love to do coverage of the country I live in and respect and value its customs and traditions. I am self-reliant and don't let anyone speak for me. At a young age, I managed to create my own brand, "Sheeth" which will soon be available worldwide. I have also authored my own book titled "I Am Sarah Muhannad." As my fans, I am proud of all of you.

From Challenges to Success

I have Tourette syndrome, which began in my childhood. With my mother’s help, I managed my condition and faced various challenges, including those in photography. I achieved my dream of launching a collection and proved my strength despite initial skepticism. I am also committed to charitable work, including donating my hair, supporting children, and helping orphans.

My Social Media Statistics

I am Sarah Muhannad, a well-known figure on social media. I started my activity on TikTok in 2018 by posting short acting clips and songs, significantly contributing to my rapid rise and widespread fame, reaching over 10 million followers worldwide. On Instagram, I have around 1.7 million followers, and on Snapchat, my account is followed by approximately 4 million people.

Launching My Own Brand

I produced amazing perfumes, driven by a significant relationship and a deep desire to create a truly memorable fragrance. I carefully selected and used unique materials, ensuring each scent was distinctive and special. I named the brand "Sheith," inspired by a word used by an anonymous admirer who sent me thoughtful gifts. In Syriac, "Sheith" means "God's gift," symbolizing a precious and cherished present.

Authored My Own Book

book published in 2022 by Dar Shaghaf. This 112-page paperback explores my personal experiences, focusing on the psychological challenges and internal struggles I faced, as well as my journey toward achieving success and overcoming difficulties. The book reflects my efforts to attain inner peace and self-confidence, offering valuable insights for teenagers dealing with similar issues.
